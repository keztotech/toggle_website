How does Toggle work?

Request

Parents or Gate Operators can effortlessly trigger a notification directly to the teacher or class coordinator for the student's school exit by tapping on the student's photo / name as soon as they enter within 100 meters of the school premises.

Prepare

Upon receiving an exit request, the assigned school representative or teacher takes on the responsibility of preparing the student to get ready for departure.

Pickup

After completing all preparations, the designated representative or teacher will guide the child to the gate for departure. Upon confirmation at the gate, the student is allowed to leave the premises.
